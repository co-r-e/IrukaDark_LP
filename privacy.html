Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2021

CORe, Inc. ("Company", "we", "us") positions the protection of personal information as a critical matter and establishes and implements the following "Personal Information Protection Policy."

1. Proper Management and Security of Personal Information

We manage personal information appropriately and strictly. We disseminate our company regulations to executives and employees and strive to ensure thorough compliance. We endeavor to prevent unauthorized access to personal information, leakage, damage, or loss of personal information, and continuously implement improvements and corrections to information security.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information

We collect and use personal information appropriately, taking into account the content and scale of our business. Specifically, we will not handle personal information beyond the scope necessary to achieve specified purposes of use and will take measures to ensure this.

3. Outsourcing and Provision of Personal Information

When outsourcing all or part of the handling of personal information, we will provide necessary and appropriate supervision to the recipient of the outsourcing to ensure the security management of the entrusted personal information. We will not disclose or provide personal information to third parties without the consent of the individual, except in the following cases:

  • When outsourcing the storage or computer processing of personal information to companies with which we have concluded confidentiality agreements
  • When it is necessary to provide personal information to financial institutions within the scope of that purpose in order to verify the validity of credit card numbers or financial account information
  • When required by law or by government order

4. Use of Analytics Tools

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"), to analyze website usage and improve our services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of this website.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity, and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. However, please note that if you do this, you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. For more information about Google Analytics and privacy, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

5. Continuous Improvement of Personal Information Protection Framework

We regularly audit and improve our personal information protection framework. We monitor changes in our business operations, social conditions, and advances in information systems, and strive to continuously improve our personal information protection framework.
